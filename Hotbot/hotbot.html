The purpose of these guidelines is to explain the use of the new brand style and to reinforce consistent application of the visual elements in all communications. This includes publications, presentations, and all other marketing materials both online and offline. Guidelines on the use of the logo are included.

Produkter

Our corporate identity is the face and personality presented to the global community. It’s as important as the products and services we provide. Our visual identity is the total effect of logo, products, brand name, trademark, advertising, brochures, and presentations— everything that represents us.

Because the brand cannot be compromised, we’ve created this guide to provide all the pertinent specifications you need to maintain its integrity. The guidelines set in this document are not meant to inhibit, but to improve the creative process. By following these guidelines, the materials you create will represent our company cohesively to the outside world.
